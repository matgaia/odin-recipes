Lasagna

Description

The lasagna is an italina dish, made of layers of ground beef, pasta, cheese and sauce

It is a grandma's dish, very common on family lunches

Ingredients

  • pasta
  • ground beef
  • cheese
  • sauce

Steps

  1. bake the pasta
  2. cook the ground beef
  3. make the sauce
  4. kake the layers of pasta, ground beef and sauce
  5. top it with cheese
  6. bake
